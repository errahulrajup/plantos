# PlantOS Product Vision

## Why PlantOS Exists
To provide a modern, open-source, metadata-driven Manufacturing Execution System (MES) that breaks the vendor lock-in of legacy systems (ABB 800xA, Siemens PCS7) while maintaining their extreme reliability and mature domain models.

## Mission
To empower process engineers to design, simulate, and execute enterprise-scale manufacturing processes without writing code, utilizing a seamless "Studio to Runtime" compilation architecture.

## Target Industries
- Food & Beverage (e.g., PlantSmör)
- Dairy
- Edible Oils
- Frozen Foods
- Fine Chemicals

## Target Customers
- Plant Managers seeking modern visualization.
- Process Engineers needing agile recipe updates.
- System Integrators building custom edge extensions.

## Commercial Strategy
**Open Core Model**:
- **Community Edition**: Core platform, Studio, Runtime, OPC-UA simulation.
- **Enterprise Edition**: High-availability clustering, Advanced AI predictive maintenance, ERP (SAP) integration, Priority Support.

## Release Roadmap
- **0.1 Alpha**: Developer Preview. Validating the architecture.
- **0.5 Beta**: Early Adopter. Running the PlantSmör Reference Plant end-to-end.
- **1.0 GA**: Commercial Release. Full Historian, Security, and Edge connectivity.
- **2.0 Industrial**: OPC-UA, Modbus, MQTT, PLC hardware integration.
- **3.0 Intelligent**: AI Digital Twin and Predictive Maintenance.
